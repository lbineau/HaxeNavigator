Simple Navigator Example (no dependencies)

Welcome to the first example of the Haxe-Navigator library.
Added to this example are 4 elements. A red, green and blue square, and a black circle. By clicking the menu, you can change the navigation state.
You can also type in a path in the URL !!!

Red Square | Green Square | Blue Square | Black Circle

But what you can also do is show two shapes at the same time, by using state cascading:
